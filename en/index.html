Mine Safety Learning Stream

Turning water-control experience into daily safety learning

Short, reviewable learning cards for mine leaders, chief engineers and water-control teams.

Clarify sources, pathways and intensity before mining

Water source, pathway and mining-induced instability are the first design questions.

Do not rely on a single water-inrush coefficient

Use structure, aquiclude, stress, mining damage and treatment effect as combined criteria.

Regional treatment needs verifiable acceptance indicators

Boreholes, grouting, pressure tests, geophysical anomalies and hydrological response form a verification chain.
